Primary Responsibilities

You can view detailed information after logging in.

1.Conduct routine patrols and respond promptly to emergencies, ensuring the safety of the public.

2.Conduct criminal investigations, gather evidence, interrogate suspects, and assist prosecutors in building cases.

3.Collaborate with other law enforcement agencies, government organizations, and community groups to address complex security issues collectively.

4.Manage and supervise subordinates, ensuring the coordination and efficiency of law enforcement operations.

5.Train new officers and continually engage in self-improvement to adapt to new challenges and technological changes.

6.Ensure that law enforcement actions comply with legal regulations and adhere to human rights and constitutional principles.

7.Familiarize and comply with law enforcement procedures, executing actions in a legal, fair, and effective manner.

8.Assist in handling major incidents, disasters, or emergency situations to ensure public safety.

9.Develop and implement crime prevention programs to enhance community safety.

Required Skills

You can view detailed information after logging in.

1.Maintaining Calmness Under Pressure :
Being able to stay calm under pressure, responding quickly and effectively to critical situations, ensuring the safety of oneself and others.

2.Tactical Thinking :
Possessing tactical thinking, making rapid situational assessments, and taking appropriate measures.

3.Effective Communication Skills :
Demonstrating good communication skills to interact effectively with colleagues, superiors, the public, and other organizations, building positive relationships.

4Coordination and Collaboration :
.Coordinating with various law enforcement agencies, departments, and community resources to collectively address complex security issues.

5.Legal Knowledge :
Proficiency in law enforcement regulations, ensuring legality during law enforcement processes and adhering to legal procedures.

6.High Professional Integrity :
Demonstrating a high level of professional integrity, adhering to professional ethics and standards, ensuring the fairness and legitimacy of law enforcement actions.

7.Effective Information Gathering:
Being able to efficiently collect intelligence, including obtaining crucial information from various sources, conducting analysis to assist in criminal investigations and prevention efforts.

8.Leadership Skills:
Demonstrating strong leadership skills, leading and commanding teams, coordinating law enforcement actions, ensuring the efficiency and safety of the team.
